The Evening Release · Step 1 of 3

Bless what was lived

The day, exactly as it unfolded, is held in light. Bless it.

Today, I lived.
The Self lived through me.
I bless what was, in all its forms.
The Evening Release · Step 2 of 3

Release the form

Whatever you held with grasping today, hand to the Self now. Open the hands.

This, or something greater.
In service of the highest Good.
Released into the keeping of the Self.
The Evening Release · Step 3 of 3

Sleep into the I AM

Three slow breaths. With each: I AM. Sleep is not absence. It is the I AM resting in itself.
